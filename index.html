About Me

My name is Moridiyah Hassan, a hopeful digital fashion illustrator who is interested in African sophistication and visual storytelling. I am inspired by the sophistication and creativity of Nigerian-based designers like Veekee James and Mimi Yina, whose work pays homage to strong silhouettes, elegance, and current femininity. I am now sharpening my skills as a digital illustrator with an interest in illustrating the elegance and richness of African fashion today.
